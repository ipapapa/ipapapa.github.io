Comparison Performance analysis in terms of QoS for wireless networks

Abstract: Our research is based on finding the optimal performance analysis of the MAC layer when QoS of Service coexists with high throughput. Give the vendor the opportunity to fully parameterize the MAC layer while achieving QoS and data rate efficiency in any network

 

An example case:

WiFi Network in which the following exist

                    • Fully Distributed (no AP)
                    • Multiclass QoS (IEEE 802.11e)
                    • High Throughput efficiency through Block-ACK
                    • Erroneous channel

 

The optimal solution can be chosen in terms of

• Flexibility
• Accuracy
• Complexity
• Depiction / Intuition
