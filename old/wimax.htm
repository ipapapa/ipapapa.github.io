Quantifying cross layer interactions in Next Generation Wireless Networks

Abstract: Our research is based on quantifying the interactions of human-oriented networking services over Next Generation Wireless Networks. A possible solution to this problem is to find the Cross layer interactions that exhibit over a multilayer system.

 

Objective: Find a closed form solution of a multilayer system

               

Challenges:

                    • Characterization of next generation applications
                    • Scheduling Algorithms
                    • Energy consumption / Mobility Issues / Mesh Mode
                    • Flexible Setting according to QoS / Traffic / Pricing
                    • Mobility Issues
