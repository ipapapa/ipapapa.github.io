Miscellaneous

Currently, I am working with IBM WebSphere Software in Application and Integration Middleware. I am part of the Emerging Technology Institute, an in the house incubator team, reporting to the CTO and Chief Strategist.

I am an alumni of the Network Performance Research Group. My primary advisor was Prof. Michael Devetsikiotis (FIEEE). Prof. Harry Perros (FIEEE) served as the co-chair of my PhD committee from the OR side.

During my undergraduate thesis, I was working in the Wireless Telecommunication Laboratory. I was advised by Dr. Georgios Paschos and Prof. Stavros Kotsopoulos.

Prof. Dimitrios Toumpakaris maintains an excellent website for Greek students that want to apply for graduate studies in the US.
